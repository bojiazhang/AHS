Welcome To American Homestay Services!

American Homestay Services(AHS) is a Council on Standards for International Education Travel (CSIET) accredited educational service provider located in Pittsburgh, PA.

Our mission is to assist high school international students embrace the opportunity to study in U.S school with position experience in a safe and nutritious environment, to provide professional homestay support service to students, parents, host families and schools.

We accomplish this by managing a large network of host families through US and having responsible local coordinators in each region that we have students.

In 2014, AHS has been accredited by CSIET (Council on Standards for International Educational Travel) to stay compliant with all CSIET standards in placing students, marketing programs, and managing homestay.

AHS works with both private and public schools to offer students abundant choices to find them a suitable school for their talent, interest and educational goals.

AHS has a marketing and sales office in Beijing, China to help facilitate international students to enroll in our program.
